Command: /pipeline-status — scans all deal files, computes weighted value per stage, flags stale deals (>14 days no update), lists 7-day action items.

Stages are not arbitrary — each has defined entry criteria and clear ownership. Dead deals get archived with documented reasons, not deleted.

Pipeline Health Metrics

MetricHealthyWarningCritical
Active deals5+3–4<3
Weighted pipeline value>$10M$5–10M<$5M
Deals in Negotiation+2+10
Partner contacts <30 days3+2<2
Dead deals this quarter<22–3>3

These metrics auto-compute from pipeline data. The /pipeline-status command generates a stage distribution table with total value, weighted value, and action items per deal. Stale deals (no update in >14 days) get flagged automatically.

Weighted value formula: deal_value × probability. A $50M deal at 30% probability = $15M weighted.

Health metrics surface problems before they compound. A pipeline with zero deals in Negotiation+ means no revenue is close — that's a red flag worth addressing immediately.
